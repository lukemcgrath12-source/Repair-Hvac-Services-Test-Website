A room temperature set point is the temperature you'd like your system to achieve before it either cycles off or continues to fan without a refrigerating effect. We recommend keeping it between 21°C and 23°C for efficient operation. The further you set it from ambient temperature, the harder and longer your system has to work to get there.

It depends on the age and condition of your system. If it's over 15 years old, parts can be hard to source, so replacement is often the better option. A general service by RepAIR HVAC Services includes:

  • Test system operation on full load and ensure adequate airflow
  • Record temperatures to determine correct refrigerant levels
  • Chemically clean return air filters, coils, grilles and fascia covers (if applicable)
  • Inspect condition of all electrical components
  • Tighten power and interconnect electrical connections
  • Flush condensate drains
  • Test zone motor operation (if applicable)

Regular maintenance extends system lifespan, improves energy efficiency and reduces the risk of breakdowns.
