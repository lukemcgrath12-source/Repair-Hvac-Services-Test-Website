What You Get

What's Included in Every Installation

We install premium Mitsubishi Electric and Daikin systems for homes and businesses — sized correctly, installed cleanly, and backed by manufacturer warranty. Here's exactly what's covered.

Site Assessment & Sizing

We attend your property, assess the space, and recommend the correctly sized system for your needs — not oversized, not undersized.

Supply & Install

We supply and install Mitsubishi Electric or Daikin systems — indoor head unit, outdoor condenser, refrigerant piping, drain, and interconnecting cables.

Clean, Tidy Workmanship

We take pride in clean installs. All pipework is neatly run and covered, penetrations are sealed, and your home is left exactly as we found it.

System Commissioning

Once installed, we commission and test the system on full load — verifying correct refrigerant charge, airflow, and electrical connections before we leave.

Manufacturer Warranty

As authorised installers, your new system comes with the full Mitsubishi Electric or Daikin manufacturer warranty — giving you long-term peace of mind.

Post-Install Support

We walk you through how to operate your system, set temperatures correctly, and remain available after installation for any questions or follow-up.

Got Questions

Installation FAQs

A standard single split system installation typically takes 3–5 hours. This includes mounting the indoor head unit, positioning the outdoor condenser, running refrigerant piping and drain lines, making electrical connections, and commissioning the system. More complex installations — such as multi-split systems or longer pipe runs — may take a full day. We'll give you a realistic timeframe when we quote.

Sizing depends on several factors — room dimensions, ceiling height, insulation, window area, and sun exposure. As a rough guide, a standard bedroom might need a 2–2.5kW unit, a medium living area 3.5–5kW, and a large open-plan space 6–9kW or more. Undersized units run constantly and never reach temperature; oversized units short-cycle and waste energy. We always assess the space before recommending a system to ensure you get the right size for your needs.
